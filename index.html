Important Rules

  • Every time you move, all baddies (monsters, super monsters, and bats) also move
  • If a baddie catches you, the game is over
  • If you fall into an abyss, the game is over
  • Baddies can also fall into abysses - use this to your advantage!
  • Plan your moves carefully and think ahead
  • Super monsters are twice as fast as regular monsters
üí°

Pro Tips

  • Keep distance from monsters - they always move towards you
  • Use walls as barriers to block monster paths
  • Bats are unpredictable, so give them extra space
  • Try to lure monsters into abysses
  • Take your time - there's no time limit!
